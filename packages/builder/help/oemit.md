# @oemit

Emit a message to everyone in your current room except yourself.
No actor attribution. Lock: `connected` — no builder flag required.

## Syntax
  `@oemit <message>`

## Examples
  `@oemit The door creaks open.`
  `@oemit A cold wind sweeps through the room.`

## See Also
  `@emit`, `say`, `pose`
