grapple firearms  -- Shooting into a grapple.

Shooting into a grapple is markedly riskier than shooting into a
normal close-combat situation.

Penalty:
  -4 pool per grappling combatant you want to avoid hitting.
  (Normal close combat is only -2 per avoided combatant.)

Why:
  Grappling combatants flail and shift unpredictably; the engine
  treats every other body in the grapple as a potential mis-hit.

Autofire:
  Autofire ignores the per-combatant penalty by design (you are
  spraying the whole pile), but every grappling combatant rolls
  Defense against the burst as normal.
