+gear weapons  -- Weapon mechanics for CoFD 2e.

Ranged Weapons:
  Damage is added as bonus successes on a hit (no successes = no bonus).
  Ranges read short/medium/long in yards. Medium = -1, long = -2.
  Initiative is a penalty to your Initiative while wielded.
  Strength below the listed minimum imposes -1 to attack rolls.
  Size: 1 = one-handed, 2 = two-handed and concealable in a coat,
        3 = two-handed and not concealable.
  Availability is the Resources cost (or Social Merit) to acquire.
  Shotguns gain 9-again. Autofire SMGs/Assault Rifles can short/medium/
  long-burst. Crossbows reload in three turns and can stake a vampire
  on a -3 attack that deals 5+ damage in one hit.

Melee Weapons:
  Always deal lethal damage. Initiative penalty applies as for ranged;
  if dual-wielding, take the higher penalty +1. Strength rules match.
  Size: 1 = hides in a hand, 2 = hides in a coat, 3+ = visible.
  Notable specials:
    Concealed   Add Size to Defense; counts as concealment vs ranged.
    Grapple     Adds the weapon bonus to your grappling pool.
    Stun        Halve victim's Size when aiming for the head to stun.
    Two-handed  Need both hands; one-handed use raises Strength req +1.

Stakes:
  Targets the heart at -3; must deal at least 5 damage in one attack.

Roller integration:
  +roll/weapon adds equipped weapon damage as bonus successes when the
  base pool yields at least 1 success. No equipped weapon = error.
