sheet willpower  -- Willpower regeneration (CoFD 2e core).

Three core regen paths drive Willpower recovery:

  Virtue trigger    Restore full Willpower when the character acts
                    on her Virtue in a meaningful scene.
  Vice indulgence   Restore +1 Willpower when the character gives
                    in to her Vice (narrative cost is on the ST).
  Full rest         A full night's rest restores all Willpower.

Commands:
  +sheet/virtue [= <reason>]    Trigger Virtue regen on yourself.
  +sheet/vice   [= <reason>]    Trigger Vice indulgence on yourself.
  +sheet/rest   [= <reason>]    Mark a full night's rest.
  +sheet/<switch> <player> [= <reason>]
                                Same regen for another player (builder+).

Limits:
  Each switch caps Willpower at the character's permanent rating
  (Resolve + Composure). Excess regen is silently dropped.

See also: sheet, virtues, vices, integrity
