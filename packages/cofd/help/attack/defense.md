attack defense  -- How Defense applies and degrades.

Defense is the standing defensive trait. It is automatically subtracted
from the attacker's pool on unarmed (/brawl), melee (/melee), and
thrown (/thrown) attacks.

Degradation:
  Each successive attack resolved against the same character within a
  single turn degrades that character's Defense by 1 for the rest of
  the turn. Defense resets at the start of the following turn.

Exceptions:
  Defense does not apply to Firearms attacks (+attack/ranged) at any
  range. Defense also does not apply to surprised or Immobilized
  targets. See help combat modifiers for the full situational table.

Reducing your own Defense:
  /allout       Lose all Defense for the turn (+2 pool).
  /charge       Lose all Defense for the turn.
  /run          -1 Defense until your next turn.
  Aiming        Defense lost mid-aim cancels the aim bonus.

See also: attack, attack damage, combat modifiers, dodge
