throw/mechanics  -- Grenade and thrown-weapon resolution.

See also: help throw (overview).

ATTACK ROLL: Dexterity + Athletics.

GRENADES (AoE)
  Aimed at the encounter zone. Every participant rolls Stamina vs the
  throw successes.
    Successes > Stamina  full force damage (lethal).
    Successes = Stamina  half force (round down).
    Successes < Stamina  evades the blast.

TILTS APPLIED
  Stun: Stunned.  Smoke: Blinded.
  Frag/Molotov: Knocked-Down when damage >= Size. Molotov also burns.

AMMO
  Grenades consumed (clip 1) on use.
