+reload  -- Reload the currently equipped firearm. Also +gear/reload.
            Reloading costs an instant action.

SYNTAX
  +reload                Reload your equipped firearm.
  +reload <weapon>       Reload a named weapon from gear.

SPEED OF RELOAD
  Magazine or speed loader:  reload without losing Defense.
  Individual rounds (tube mag, revolver):  no Defense this turn.

AUTOFIRE COSTS
  Short burst  3 rounds   Medium burst  10 rounds
  Long burst   20 rounds  Covering fire 10 rounds

If no spare ammo, the command fails. Use +gear to inspect counts.
