integrity outcomes  -- Success tiers and Conditions on a Breaking Point.

Each result tier of the Resolve + Composure roll has a different
narrative and mechanical fallout.

  Dramatic failure  Lose 1 Integrity. Gain Broken, Fugue, or Madness.
                    +1 Beat.
  Failure           Lose 1 Integrity. Gain Shaken or Guilty.
  Success           No loss. Gain Guilty, Shaken, or Spooked.
  Exceptional       No loss. Gain Steadfast or Inspired. Restore
                    1 Willpower. +1 Beat.

Condition selection:
  The engine grants the first Condition from the outcome list that is
  not already active on the character. If every candidate is already
  on the sheet, it falls back to the first entry on the list.

  Conditions feed into +condition. They resolve normally (1 Beat on
  resolution) and follow the standard persistent-vs-resolved rules.

See also: integrity, condition, beat
