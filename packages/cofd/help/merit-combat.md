merit-combat  -- Combat-relevant merits wired into the dice engine.

These merits modify rolls automatically when the relevant action fires.
+roll, +attack, and +combat/init read them from your sheet.

MERITS
  Fast Reflexes (1-3)     +dots to Initiative result.
  Quick Draw (1)          Drawing equipped weapon costs no first
                          action, even on Slow weapons. Instanced
                          per weapon class (Firearms, Melee, ...).
  Killer Instinct (1-3)   +dots vs Beaten Down or >half-Health.
  Iron Stamina (1-3)      Reduce own wound penalty by dots (min 0).
  Heavy Hitter (3)        +1 raw hits on successful melee, pre-armor.

For storage key syntax under +sheet/set, see merit-combat/storage.

SEE ALSO: help merits, help attack, help combat, help roll
