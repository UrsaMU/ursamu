roll/expressions  -- Dice-pool expression grammar.

See also: help roll (overview).

FORMS
  Strength+Brawl               Attribute + Skill.
  Dexterity+Crafts/Automotive  Skill with specialty.
  Wits+Composure+2             Combo with numeric modifier.
  Vigor                        Single trait (works for powers).
  6                            Raw dice pool.

NAMES RECOGNIZED
  9 Attributes, 24 Skills, the active template's Power Stat (and
  aliases like bp, pu), Morality trait, and valid Powers (vigor,
  auspex, forces, etc).
