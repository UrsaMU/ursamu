roll/successes  -- Counting, n-again, chance dice, outcomes.

See also: help roll (overview).

SUCCESS AND AGAIN
  Success: any die showing 8, 9, or 10.
  10-again: tens reroll and chain. 9-again / 8-again lower the bar.

ROTE: reroll every initial failure once. Obeys n-again; no rote loop.

CHANCE DIE: pool <= 0 rolls a single d10. Success only on 10. A 1 =
Dramatic Failure. Ignores /rote, /9again, /8again.

OUTCOMES
  Exceptional: 5+ successes -> Inspired (or template equivalent).

PENALTIES
  Wound: auto-applied from Health. Raw pools (+roll 6) skip.
  Untrained: Mental -3, Physical/Social -1.
