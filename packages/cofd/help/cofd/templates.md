cofd/templates  -- Supernatural templates and overlays.

See also: help cofd (overview).

Templates available:
  Mortal      Base human template.
  Changeling  The Lost — overlays Clarity (Morality), Wyrd (Power),
              Glamour (Energy), Seeming, Kith, Court, Needle, Thread.

Each template adjusts the sheet display, validation, and energy pool.
See help cg for the chargen flow that applies these.
