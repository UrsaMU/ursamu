social force  -- Forcing the doors: a high-risk one-shot.

Forced doors (CoFD 2e p.83) collapse the entire maneuver into a
single roll: Manipulation + Persuasion vs the subject's Composure,
penalised by every remaining door.

Pool penalty:
  -1 die per remaining door. A subject with 4 doors still standing
  imposes -4 on the forcing roll.

Result tiers:
  Exceptional success    Maneuver succeeds. Subject capitulates.
  Success                Maneuver succeeds with strain.
  Failure                Maneuver ends. Soft leverage cannot recover it.
  Dramatic failure       Maneuver ends and the subject is permanently
                         immune from your further attempts.

Use /force when time pressure makes the slow drip of door rolls
untenable. The risk is real: a failure ends the entire maneuver and
all the leverage you have spent is gone.

See also: social, social doors
