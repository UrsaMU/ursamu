social doors  -- Doors and contested door rolls.

Doors are the subject's mental defences against persuasion. Knocking
each one down brings the subject closer to capitulation.

Base count:
  Doors base = min(subject.Resolve, subject.Composure).

Door roll (+social/door):
  Contested Manipulation + Persuasion vs Composure. The subject's
  Composure dice are rolled by the engine on the subject's behalf;
  ST may override the result if the subject is an NPC.

Cumulative penalty:
  Failed door rolls accumulate a cumulative -1 penalty (CoFD 2e p.82).
  The penalty persists for the duration of the maneuver and is shown
  in +social/status.

Result tiers:
  Exceptional success    Opens two doors.
  Success                Opens one door.
  Failure                Adds -1 to all future door rolls.
  Dramatic failure       Ends the maneuver. The subject becomes
                         permanently immune from your further attempts.

When the last door falls the subject capitulates and the maneuver
resolves successfully.

See also: social, social impression, social leverage
