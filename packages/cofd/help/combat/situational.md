combat situational  -- Prone, into-melee, Willpower, surprise modifiers.

Prone target:
  Ranged or thrown attack      -2
  Close-combat attack          +2

Shooting into close combat (single shot):
  Per avoided combatant        -2
  Per grappling combatant      -4
  Does not apply to autofire.

Surprise and Immobilized:
  Surprised or immobilized target   Defense does not apply.

Willpower:
  Willpower spend              +3 dice (or +2 to one Resistance trait
                               for one roll, player's choice).

Firearms in close combat:
  Firearms in close combat     target Defense raised by gun Size + 1.

These modifiers stack with the base attack modifiers, range, and
concealment in the natural way. Mid-attack switches like /aim and
/allout fold in automatically through their +attack switches.
