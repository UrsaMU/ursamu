combat/initiative  -- Initiative scores and what affects them.

See also: help combat (overview).

FORMULA
  Init = 1d10 + Dexterity + Composure + weapon Init modifier
  Weapon mod usually negative (sword -2, rifle -4). Drop or sling to
  remove the penalty.

DUAL-WIELD
  Larger penalty, then -1 more. Crowbar(-2) + shield(-4) = -5.

AMBUSH (pre-init): Attacker Dex+Stealth vs Defender Wits+Composure.
  Loser: no action or Defense on turn 1.

TIES
  Break by Composure, then Dexterity, then ST or roll-off.

For Defense degradation and delaying, see combat/defense and
combat/delaying.
