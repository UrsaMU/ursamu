combat range  -- Range modifiers for ranged attacks.

Each ranged weapon has three range bands printed on its catalog
entry. The engine reads the band the target sits in and applies the
penalty to the attacker's pool.

  Short range                  no penalty
  Medium range                 -1
  Long range                   -2
  Beyond long range            chance die only

Beyond long range:
  Any shot past the weapon's long-range value drops the pool to a
  chance die regardless of attacker traits. A chance die ignores
  rote and 9-again/8-again.

The engine does not currently track per-target range bands; the
attacker declares the band in the room as part of the +attack
narrative and the ST applies the modifier through the situational
modifier on the +attack command line.
