vices  -- The Vice anchor in CoFD 2e.

A character's Vice is her short-term comfort: the easy way out.
Acting on Vice restores one Willpower point, once per scene.

Set in chargen Stage 1 via +cg/set vice=<name> (case-insensitive).

CATALOG
  Ambitious     Wants advancement beyond what she has earned. WP:
                jockeys for position instead of useful action.
  Arrogant      Defines himself by being better than others. WP:
                lords his superiority over someone.
  Competitive   Needs to win; may cheat. WP: indulges in a competition
                that distracts from more pressing matters.
  Greedy        Hoards resources for himself. WP: takes an advantage
                for himself, denying it to another character.

Competitive appears as both Virtue and Vice; pick one, not both.
