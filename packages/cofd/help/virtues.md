virtues  -- The Virtue anchor in CoFD 2e.

A character's Virtue is who she is at her best. Acting on Virtue is
hard; doing so restores all spent Willpower, once per scene.

Set in chargen Stage 1 via +cg/set virtue=<name> (case-insensitive).

CATALOG
  Competitive   Pushes to outpace rivals through honest effort. WP:
                competing honestly with someone who outclasses her.
  Generous      Gives freely, expects nothing back. WP: deprived of
                an important resource or risked because of his nature.
  Just          Driven by fairness; sticks her neck out for it. WP:
                sets her goals aside to see justice done.
  Loyal         Devoted to a person or group. WP: refusing to act
                against group interests puts him at risk.

Competitive appears as both Virtue and Vice; pick one, not both.
